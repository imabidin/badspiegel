Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about our products, orders, payments, shipping, and more. If your question is not covered here, feel free to call us (Mon–Fri: 8:00 AM–4:00 PM) or send us an email.

Products

Can I place an order by phone?

Unfortunately, no. Due to the variety of options (custom dimensions, features), please order only via our website and carefully review your order confirmation.

Can I place an order in person?

Yes, you can. We’re open Mon–Fri from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. You can pay on-site (e.g., by debit card) and pick up your items if you wish.

I didn't receive an order confirmation. What should I do?

Please check your spam folder first. If it’s not there, call or email us so we can verify whether your order was received.

Payment

Which payment methods are available?

All information regarding payment methods can be found on our Payment Methods page.

Why did I receive two invoices (Klarna & Our Company)?

If you chose Klarna Invoice, you only pay Klarna. Our invoice is just for documentation. Please do NOT transfer money to us, as you may incur late fees if the payment isn’t sent to Klarna.

Sofortüberweisung is now Klarna Sofort—what does that mean?

Sofort AG was acquired by Klarna. The functionality remains the same. During checkout, just select "Klarna Sofort" as your payment method.

Shipping & Delivery

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping for mirrors within Germany is usually free of charge (except TV mirrors). For deliveries within the EU, a €49.99 fee applies. Surcharges for oversized goods (mirror cabinets, TV mirrors) may apply.

What does "curbside delivery" mean?

The shipment will be delivered to the sidewalk or property boundary. For heavy mirrors, please arrange for a second person to help carry them.

Do you deliver to Packstations?

Unfortunately, no, due to the size and handling of mirrors and mirror cabinets.

Additional Topics

When should I hire a professional installer?

We recommend doing so only after receiving and checking your items to ensure everything is complete and undamaged.

What if there’s transport damage?

Please document the damage (photos, order number) and contact us immediately via email at complaints@example.com. We will find a quick and simple solution for you.

Can I use an Amazon voucher here?

No, that’s not possible. Amazon vouchers are only valid for use on Amazon websites.

How can I save my product configuration?

Simply create a customer account and save your mirror configuration in the cart. You can compare or remove configurations at any time.
