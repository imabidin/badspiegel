Shipping Conditions

Depending on size and weight, your order will be shipped via DHL or freight forwarding (Spedition). You’ll receive a shipping confirmation by email as soon as your package or pallet leaves our warehouse.

Shipping via DHL

Smaller and medium-sized parcels (up to 31.5 kg or a girth of 360 cm) are shipped with DHL. Generally, delivery takes place Monday through Saturday directly to your doorstep. However, very large parcels may not be eligible for delivery to a Packstation or post office.

Shipping via Freight Forwarder (Spedition)

Items that exceed 31.5 kg or the maximum parcel dimensions are shipped via freight forwarding. Delivery is “free curbside,” meaning the goods are delivered to the sidewalk or property boundary. The freight company typically schedules the delivery date via email or phone, offering a 4-hour time window (Mon–Fri, 8:00–16:00). Saturday, Sunday, and public holiday deliveries are not possible.

Important: If you cannot be at home during the scheduled delivery, please let us know in advance. We can reschedule if possible. If delivery cannot be completed within one week (storage period), the shipment will be returned to us. In such cases, we will have to charge you for any additional shipping costs for the next delivery attempt.

Transport Damage & Acceptance

Please inspect your items upon arrival for any visible damage. If you notice damage to the packaging or the goods themselves, please note this clearly “with reservation” on the delivery slip or refuse to accept the shipment if necessary. Then contact us immediately so we can resolve the issue.

Delivery Times

Unless otherwise stated in the product description, delivery within Germany takes 3 business days, and international orders take up to 12 business days after order confirmation (or after payment receipt for prepayment).

Please note that no deliveries are made on Sundays or public holidays. If a public holiday falls on a weekday (Mon–Fri), the delivery time may be extended accordingly.

Right of Withdrawal: In the event of a withdrawal, the reimbursement of outbound shipping costs is limited to standard shipping (e.g., €9.99).
