Payment Methods

Safe and convenient payment: During checkout, you can choose from a variety of payment methods. Below, you will find all the details about our payment options.

Jump directly to:

  • Prepayment (Bank Transfer)
  • PayPal
  • Klarna
  • Credit Card
  • iDeal / Apple Pay / Google Pay

Please note that for international shipments, only prepayment, PayPal, or Klarna (depending on availability in your country) can be used.

Prepayment (Bank Transfer)

When choosing prepayment, you transfer the invoice amount to our bank account using the order number as reference. You will receive our bank details in a separate email after completing your purchase.

Please note that items not in stock and requiring custom production will only enter production after we have received your payment. The delivery time is calculated from the date your payment is received.

PayPal incl. Invoice Purchase, Credit Card, Direct Debit

With PayPal, you can pay for your order quickly and securely. After checkout, you’ll be redirected to PayPal. There, you can pay with your PayPal balance or – even without a PayPal account – by invoice, direct debit, or credit card.

PayPal Invoice Purchase: For orders up to €1,500 (subject to a credit check), new customers can also pay by invoice through PayPal. You will receive your invoice from PayPal and have 30 days to complete the payment. Please note that PayPal may request payment after the product has been shipped, even if you haven’t received your item yet (e.g., due to longer production times for custom-made products).

PayPal also offers Buyer Protection for additional security on every transaction.

Klarna: Invoice, Installments & Sofort

Klarna Invoice Purchase: Buy now and pay your invoice within 14 to 30 days (depending on the respective Klarna terms). Ordered items will enter production or be shipped as soon as we receive Klarna’s approval. Please note that your billing and shipping addresses must match.

Klarna Installments: Split your invoice into flexible installments. The payment process is handled directly by Klarna. You will receive an email from Klarna with your invoice after your order has been shipped. Please do not transfer the amount to our bank account; only pay directly to Klarna.

Klarna Sofort (formerly Sofortüberweisung): If you have an online banking account with PIN/TAN functionality, you can pay directly during checkout. Once confirmed, the payment is transferred immediately, allowing us to process your order as soon as possible.

Credit Card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. All data is transmitted securely over an encrypted connection. Depending on your card provider, an additional 3D Secure authentication (Verified by Visa / Mastercard Identity Check) may be required to confirm your payment.

iDeal, Apple Pay & Google Pay

iDeal: As one of the most popular payment methods in the Netherlands, iDeal enables direct and secure transfers from your online banking account.

Apple Pay: Easily make payments on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac using Face ID, Touch ID, or your device code. Your order is processed immediately.

Google Pay: Use your Android device or Chrome browser for quick and secure checkout. Payment is also processed instantly.
